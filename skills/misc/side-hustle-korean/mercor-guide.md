## Application Steps

1. Go to https://work.mercor.com/explore
2. Upload resume (use template below)
3. Complete AI interview (based on your resume)
4. Pass test task (writing/analysis)
5. Get access to project board

**Key:** Treat application seriously. Most rejections come from vague writing or missed instructions.

## Interview Tips

1. **Be specific** — not "I'm good at languages" but "I translated a 2000-word Korean academic paper with 99% accuracy"
2. **Show judgment** — Mercor tests your ability to evaluate AI output quality
3. **Write clearly** — strong writing = strong AI training signal
4. **Don't rush** — AI interview adapts to your answers, take time
