## Tips for First 5 Orders

1. **Price low, deliver fast** — $5 orders with 12hr delivery = reviews
2. **Over-deliver slightly** — add a free revision or brief note in Korean
3. **Respond to messages within 1 hour** — response time affects ranking
4. **Ask happy clients for reviews** — "If you're satisfied, a 5-star review helps me a lot!"
5. **After 5 reviews, raise prices** — Move Basic to $10, Standard to $20
